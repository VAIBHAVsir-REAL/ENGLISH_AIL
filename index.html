📍 Destination

📅 Itinerary Timeline

  • 🗓 Day 1 & 2 (15–16 July): Train journey from Delhi → Ernakulam. Assemble 3:30 PM, board at 5:00 PM. Dinner onboard, overnight journey.
  • 🗓 Day 3 (17 July): Arrive 8:00 AM, check-in, breakfast. Marari Beach visit, sunset photos, dinner at resort.
  • 🗓 Day 4 (18 July): Houseboat cruise with lunch, village walk, visit to Karumadikuttan Buddha Statue. Campfire night.
  • 🗓 Day 5 (19 July): Visit Ambalappuzha Krishna Temple, palpayasam, coir-making demo, market walk, farewell Sadya dinner.
  • 🗓 Day 6 & 7 (20–21 July): Early breakfast, board return train at 8:00 AM. Arrive Delhi 21 July by noon. Dispersal with memories!
